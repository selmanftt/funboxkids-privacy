Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

FunBox Kids is designed for children ages 3+. We are fully committed to protecting children's privacy and comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).

1. Information We Collect

FunBox Kids does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users, including children under 13. Specifically, we do not collect: names, email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses, photos or videos, location data, device identifiers for tracking purposes, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

FunBox Kids is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Our app is designed as a safe, child-friendly environment with no social features, no user accounts, and no user-generated content.

3. Advertising

FunBox Kids displays contextual, non-personalized advertisements through Google AdMob. All advertisements are configured for child-directed treatment, meaning they are age-appropriate and family-safe. We do not serve personalized or behaviorally targeted ads. Ad settings include: non-personalized ads only (npa=1), restricted data processing (rdp=1), child-directed treatment enabled, and maximum ad content rating set to "General".

4. In-App Purchases

FunBox Kids offers optional auto-renewable subscriptions (Premium) that remove ads and unlock additional games. All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store and are subject to Apple's privacy policy. We do not directly process or store any payment information.

5. Data Storage

Game progress (such as scores and daily rewards) is stored locally on the device using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves the device and is not transmitted to any server.

6. Third-Party Services

FunBox Kids uses Google AdMob for advertising. Google AdMob may collect certain device information for ad serving purposes, but all data collection is configured for COPPA compliance and child-directed treatment. For more information, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

7. External Links

FunBox Kids does not contain any external links. All navigation to external websites is blocked within the app to ensure children's safety.

8. Parental Controls

FunBox Kids includes a parent section accessible through the settings icon. Parents can manage sound settings and review subscription options.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage parents and guardians to review this policy periodically.
